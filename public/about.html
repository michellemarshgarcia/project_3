About Kathy's Cookies

Kathy Marsh lives in beautiful Perry Park, Colorado with her husband of fifty years. Kathy has long enjoyed baking, she began to focus on custom sugar cookie creations with the arrival of her three granddaughters. Kathy hails from a long line of professional bakers and continues to build her technique and expand her repertoire through regular study, collaboration with other cookie artists and kitchen experimentation.

Awards

Douglas County Fair Summer 2017:

Succulent Cookies awarded both First Place Blue Ribbon and Superintendant Award. Judge’s comments: “Fabulous! Extremely unique, beautiful presentation, good technique using tips, design adheres to frosting, nice texture, excellent flavor, outstanding exhibit.”

Wildlife Cookies plus Colorado flag awarded First Place Blue Ribbon. Judge’s comments: “Artistic, tasty, beautiful flag decoration, nice glaze on animals.”
